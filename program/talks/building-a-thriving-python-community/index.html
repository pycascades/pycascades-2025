Building a thriving Python Community: From Workshops to Masterminds

Kafui Alordo

From a volunteer coach to leading the Python User Group Ho, this talk document my journey of building a vibrant Python community in Ghana. I'll share insights on overcoming challenges, fostering collaboration, and creating a supportive environment for Python enthusiasts at all levels. Discovering how cultivating a culture of curiosity, continuous learning, and active community participation can accelerate both personal and professional growth.

Exploring strategies to transform local Python workshops into thriving masterminds that drive innovation and empower the developer ecosystem.

About Kafui Alordo

Kafui is a Software Engineer and a Developer Advocate with Cradx, Kumasi, Ghana. He worked a previous role as a Systems Support Specialist at CLODS Inc. Kafui is the cofounder of Adomi Health and the Lead/Organizer of PyHo - a volunteer run Python Community in Ho, Volta Region, Ghana. He is also a Django Software Foundation individual member and Django Girls Ho Lead/organizer - through which he is actively building his developer community in the capacity of diversity and inclusion. Outside coding and community development, Kafui spends time at the gym to keep fit, goes for hiking, read books and enjoys alone time by taking a long walk down town.
